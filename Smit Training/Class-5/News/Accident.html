3 Pak Children Accidently Get Locked In Trunk, Suffocate To Death: Report

According to initial reports, the three siblings--2-year-old Zohan, 6-year-old Saira, and 7-year-old Faria--were suffocated to death.

Rawalpindi, Pakistan: Three minor siblings suffocated to death after accidentally locking themselves inside a trunk in Pakistan's Rawalpindi, ARY News reported.

ARY News is a Pakistani news channel.

As per the details, the incident took place in the Shah Khalid Khaluni area of Rawalpindi, wherein three minor siblings--two girls and a boy--accidentally locked themselves inside a trunk while playing.
